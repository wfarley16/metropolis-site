1 · Criteria first

Figure out what you want

A guided discovery interview turns “I guess I want something better” into a durable criteria profile — the work itself, growth, compensation, people, place, dealbreakers. You own it, refine it, and reuse it for every decision that follows.

2 · Explained matches

Rankings you can interrogate

Every role is ranked against your stated criteria, with the reasoning shown — where it fits, where it falls short, what we couldn't verify. No black-box fit score.

3 · Depth over volume

Five applications you believe in

Instead of a hundred lottery tickets: a shortlist you can defend, and a real plan for each — research, tailoring, timing, follow-ups — tracked in one living record.
